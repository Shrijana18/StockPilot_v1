3. **Use cursor highlighting** to show clicks clearly

2. **Background music** - optional, keep it subtle
3. **No background noise** - record in quiet environment

- Keep each scene concise
- Total video: 2-3 minutes maximum
- Meta reviewers are busy - respect their time
