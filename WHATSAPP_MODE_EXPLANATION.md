## How to Upgrade

1. Go to **Profile Settings** ‚Üí **WhatsApp Business**
2. Scroll to **Advanced Settings**
3. Change **WhatsApp Provider** from "Direct Link" to:
   - **Meta WhatsApp Business API** (for official API)
   - **Twilio WhatsApp API** (for third-party)
4. Enter your API credentials
5. Click "Test Connection"
6. Done! All messages will now send automatically
