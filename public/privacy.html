Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 16, 2025

FLYP ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

Information We Collect

  • Personal information you provide during registration (e.g., name, email, phone)
  • Business-related data such as inventory, invoices, and transaction history
  • Usage data and analytics to improve our services

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your FLYP account
  • To provide features such as billing, inventory, analytics, and distributor connections
  • To improve performance, personalize content, and communicate updates

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share limited data with trusted third-party services strictly for delivering our features (e.g., Firebase, analytics, cloud functions).

Data Security

All your data is encrypted and stored securely using industry-standard protocols and Google Firebase infrastructure.

Account and Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

How to Request Account Deletion:

  1. Send an email to support@flypnow.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request"
  2. Include your registered phone number or email address in the request
  3. Specify if you want to delete your entire account or only specific data
  4. We will process your request within 30 days

What Data Will Be Deleted:

  • Your account profile and personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Order history and transaction data
  • Saved addresses and payment methods
  • App preferences and settings

Data Retention:

Some data may be retained for legal or business purposes as required by law, including:

  • Financial transaction records (for tax and accounting purposes)
  • Data required for fraud prevention and security
  • Anonymized analytics data that cannot be linked to your identity

All retained data will be kept securely and used only for the purposes stated above.

Contact

If you have any questions or requests regarding your data, please contact us at support@flypnow.com.

By using FLYP, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
