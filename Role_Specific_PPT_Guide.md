## Design Recommendations

### Color Scheme
- **Primary:** Green/Teal gradient (FLYP brand colors)
- **Background:** Dark theme or light theme
- **Accent:** White text with gradient highlights

### Typography
- **Headings:** Bold, 36-44pt
- **Body:** Regular, 18-24pt
- **Fonts:** Montserrat, Poppins, Inter

### Visual Elements
- **Screenshots:** Use actual FLYP app screenshots
- **Icons:** Consistent icon set (Font Awesome, Material Icons)
- **Charts:** Data visualizations for statistics
- **Diagrams:** Flow charts and network diagrams

## Presentation Tips

### For Product Manufacturers
1. **Emphasize:** Visibility, control, data-driven decisions
2. **Focus on:** Distributor performance, market insights
3. **Highlight:** Strategic benefits, competitive advantage
4. **Show:** Network diagrams, analytics dashboards

### For Distributors
1. **Emphasize:** Automation, time savings, efficiency
2. **Focus on:** Retailer management, order processing
3. **Highlight:** WhatsApp automation, employee management
4. **Show:** Workflow diagrams, automation examples

### For Retailers
1. **Emphasize:** Ease of use, modern features, customer service
2. **Focus on:** POS system, inventory management
3. **Highlight:** Voice billing, distributor connections
4. **Show:** POS interface, customer management features

## Customization Tips

### Adapt to Your Audience
- **Small businesses:** Focus on ease of use and affordability
- **Large businesses:** Emphasize scalability and enterprise features
- **Tech-savvy:** Highlight AI and automation
- **Traditional:** Focus on practical benefits and ROI

### Industry-Specific
- **FMCG:** Emphasize inventory and distributor management
- **Electronics:** Focus on product tracking and analytics
- **Food & Beverage:** Highlight inventory and expiry management
- **General Retail:** Focus on POS and customer management
