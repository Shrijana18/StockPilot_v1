# PART 1: PRODUCT MANUFACTURERS

## What is FLYP for Product Manufacturers?

FLYP is a comprehensive supply chain visibility and management platform designed specifically for product manufacturers who want complete control and insight into their distribution network. It connects you directly with your distributors, giving you real-time visibility into how your products move from manufacturing to end customers.

### The Manufacturer's Challenge

As a product manufacturer, you face unique challenges:

**1. Limited Visibility**
- You manufacture products but lose track once they leave your warehouse
- No real-time data on which distributors are performing
- Can't see which regions are selling well
- Don't know when distributors need restocking

**2. Manual Relationship Management**
- Managing distributor relationships through phone calls and emails
- No centralized system to track distributor performance
- Difficult to identify top-performing vs. underperforming distributors
- No automated way to share product catalogs and updates

**3. Lack of Data-Driven Decisions**
- No analytics on product performance across regions
- Can't forecast demand accurately
- Don't know which products are popular where
- Missing insights to optimize production

**4. Communication Gaps**
- Distributors place orders through various channels (phone, WhatsApp, email)
- No standardized order process
- Difficult to track order status
- Delayed information about market trends

**5. Inventory Management Issues**
- Don't know distributor stock levels
- Can't predict when distributors will reorder
- Risk of overproduction or stockouts
- No visibility into end-customer demand
